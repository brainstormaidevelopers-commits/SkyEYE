# BRAINStØrm R&D Roadmap: Scientific & Geopolitical Nuances

This document serves as a long-term research and development guide for the SkyEye platform, focusing on the intersection of planetary physics, sensor fusion, and global transparency.

## 1. Scientific & Technical R&D

### Temporal Gaussian Hierarchy (TGH) Optimization
- **Multiscale Temporal Hierarchies**: Researching how to weigh a 10-minute GOES weather update against a 30-meter Landsat thermal image from 6 hours ago.
- **Predictive Interpolation**: Training TGH to predict seasonal vegetation or urban growth patterns to "hallucinate" high-confidence details between satellite passes.

### Physics-Guided Reconstruction (Genesis)
- **Differentiable Simulation Gradients**: Using Genesis to calculate error gradients between a 3D reconstruction and original sensor data. If a reconstructed building's shadow doesn't match the satellite imagery, the system "backpropagates" to adjust vertex height.
- **Structural Integrity Inference**: Developing AI models that infer the internal structural health of infrastructure (e.g., bridges, dams) by combining SAR surface deformation with physical stress models.

### Fractal-Aware Scale Invariance (Dither3D)
- **Natural Detail Signatures**: Researching how to maintain the specific "fractal dimension" of different features (e.g., the branching of rivers vs. the grid density of slums) during LOD transitions.
- **Anti-Hallucination Constraints**: Ensuring that procedural fractal detail remains grounded in the lowest-resolution verifiable data points.

## 2. Geopolitical & Political R&D

### Verification & The "Truth" Pipeline
- **Multi-Sensor Cross-Validation**: Creating a "Trust Score" for events. For example, a "fire" is only verified if the Visual, Thermal, and Chemical (CO2 spike) sensors all confirm the anomaly.
- **Anti-Deepfake Protocol**: Using cryptographically signed metadata from trusted sources (journalists, government APIs) as anchor points for reconstructions.

### Privacy & Ethical Boundaries
- **Automated Anonymization**: Real-time identification and blurring of faces, private license plates, and non-public interiors in high-res reconstructions.
- **"The Right to Peek" vs. "The Right to Hide"**: Developing a policy framework for non-permissive areas (conflict zones vs. residential neighborhoods).

### Dual-Use & National Security
- **Critical Infrastructure Safeguards**: Methods to obfuscate sensitive military or power-grid details while maintaining a coherent planetary model for research.
- **Export Control Logic**: Implementing territory-based restrictions for specific sensor layers (e.g., sub-meter SAR in active conflict zones).

## 3. UI/UX: Sensory Expansion Interface

### Cognitive Load Management
- **The Sensory Switcher**: A simplified 6-slot interface (Vision, Heat, Echo, Air, Earth, Flow) that maps to satellite and sensor types.
- **4D Scrubbing**: Intuitive time-travel UI that allows users to "peek" behind the temporal blinds of history.
